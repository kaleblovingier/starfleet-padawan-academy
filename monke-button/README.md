# MONKE BUTTON™

## "Make Monke Not Kill Monke"

**One button. Infinite peace. Zero monke-on-monke violence.**

The Monke Button is the ultimate de-escalation device for a world (or jungle, or starship) full of monkes.

When monkes get too monke — swinging, screeching, biting, launching poo, or generally turning the place into a battlefield — you press the button.

**Result:** Monke no longer kill monke. Monke now groom monke. Monke share banana. Monke vibe.

## How It Works (Patent Pending)

1. **Detect** — Sensors (or just vibes) notice rising monke aggression levels.
2. **Press** — Any monke (or enlightened non-monke) slams the big red button.
3. **Transform** — The button emits a powerful "not kill" field (scientifically it's probably just a bright light + banana scent + subliminal grooming sounds).
4. **Peace** — All monkes in range immediately enter "chill monke" state. Fists unclench. Teeth retract. Bananas are offered instead of thrown.

**Warning:** After peace, monkes often become **tired and hungry**. See "Monke Tired Hungry" section below for care protocols. The button now supports FEED and REST modes.

## Monke Tired Hungry Phase

Pressing the Monke Button stops the killing... but monke gets exhausted and hangry.

**Symptoms:**
- Slumped posture
- Sad eyes
- Empty banana peels everywhere
- Low energy, high grump potential if not addressed

**Solution (use the Monke Button in care mode):**
- **FEED MONKE**: Offer bananas, rations, or academy protein packs. Restores energy.
- **LET MONKE REST**: Allow nap, grooming session, or quiet time in the leaves / Starship bunk.
- Both actions required for full recovery.

After proper care, monke enters **"Monke Happy and Full"** state: content, well-rested, ready for more (peaceful) monke activities or training.

In the **Starfleet Padawan Academy**, this phase is critical after "Punch the Glass" drills or high-stress Monke Button activations. Cadets learn that true mastery includes caring for the monke inside (and around) you. Tired hungry monke = bad decisions. Fed rested monke = strong with the Force and the rockets.

**Modes:**
- **Classic Monke** — Basic peace field. Works on chimps, humans who forgot how to be civilized, crypto degens, and rowdy academy cadets.
- **Academy Monke** (Starfleet Padawan Edition) — Integrates with The Living Force training. Combines the button press with lightsaber form breathing to convert raw primal energy into focused, peaceful power. Used during "Punch the Glass" drills and Forge sessions when things get too spicy.
- **Hard Monke** — For when monkes have really gone off the rails. Requires two hands and a banana offering.
