- Confirmed hostile. Not drills. Not glitches.

## The Inbound Protocol (How to Survive)

1. **Scan & Classify** — Radar lights up. "BOGIES INBOUND. MULTIPLE. CLOSING."
2. **Calm the Monke** — Deploy Monke Button immediately. Peace first. Then feed. Then rest. A hangry monke is a dead monke.
3. **Drop into the Loop** — Neverender sync. Let the Tame Impala waves carry the targeting solution. Let the Justice pulses mark the beat.
4. **Wait for the Cue** — The Right on Cue sweeper is your only friend. Green zone = weapons free.
5. **Engage** — Lock. Release. Clean. No early shots. No late shots. The glass shatters only when it must.
6. **Debrief** — After-action with the Council. The monkes that were inbound threats are now neutralized. The loop continues.
