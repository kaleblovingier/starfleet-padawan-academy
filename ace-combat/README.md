## The Ace Combat Protocol (How to Survive the Inbound)

1. **Scan & Classify** — Radar lights up. "BOGIES INBOUND. MULTIPLE. CLOSING FAST."
2. **Calm the Monke** — Deploy Monke Button immediately. Peace. Feed. Rest. A hangry or raging monke is a dead monke (and a missed lock).
3. **Drop into the Loop** — Neverender sync. Let the Tame Impala waves carry your targeting solution. Let the Justice pulses mark the beat.
4. **Wait for the Cue** — The Right on Cue sweeper is your only friend. Green zone = weapons free.
5. **Engage** — Lock. Fox 2 (heat seeker). Fox 3 (radar missile). Guns if needed. Clean. No early. No late. The glass shatters only when it must.
6. **Splash & Debrief** — "Splash one." "All inbound neutralized." After-action with the Council. The monkes that were threats are now at peace. The loop continues.
